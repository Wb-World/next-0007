Professional Excellence

Since our inception in 2015, Anu&Co has been at the forefront of the home care industry. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide families with reliable, verified, and compassionate care that they can trust implicitly.

With years of hands on experience, we have refined our processes, expanded our service offerings, and built a network of professionals who are not just skilled, but also deeply committed to their calling. Our expertise spans across specialized nursing, infant care, and personalized home maintenance.

Our experience is reflected in the thousands of families we have served across India. We understand the nuances of home care, from the delicate needs of a newborn to the complex requirements of post-operative patient recovery.
