Baby & Infant Care

Our certified infant caregivers are trained in all aspects of newborn and toddler development — from establishing healthy feeding and sleep routines to providing age-appropriate stimulation. Every caregiver holds an infant first-aid certificate and undergoes specialized training in newborn hygiene, cry interpretation, and safe handling.

  • 24/7 newborn care including night-duty shifts
  • Feeding schedules, bottle prep, and breastfeeding support
  • Developmental play, tummy time, and sensory activities
  • Infant CPR and first-aid certified caregivers
  • Toddler care up to 5 years — including toilet training
  • Regular progress updates and parent communication

Patient Care Attendant

Recovering at home is the most comfortable option — but it requires skilled support. Our trained patient care attendants bridge the gap between hospital-grade care and home comfort, working under your physician's guidance to ensure safe, monitored, and dignified recovery.

  • Post-surgical and post-hospitalization recovery support
  • Medication administration and schedule adherence
  • Vital signs monitoring: BP, glucose, pulse, temperature
  • Wound care, catheter management, and tube feeding
  • Mobility assistance and physiotherapy exercise support
  • Daily health logs shared with family and physicians

Certified Home Nursing

Our panel of B.Sc and GNM-qualified nurses, all registered with the Indian Nursing Council, bring clinical excellence to your doorstep. They handle complex medical procedures with the same professionalism and hygiene standards as a hospital, in the comfort and privacy of your home.

  • Injections, IV cannulation, and infusion therapy
  • Blood glucose, BP, and SpO₂ monitoring
  • Post-operative wound care and surgical dressing changes
  • Nasogastric tube care and catheter management
  • Palliative and end-of-life care support
  • Coordination with attending specialists and physicians
