Our Story

Building India's Most
Trusted Care Network

Anu&Co was founded in 2015 by Anupama Rao, a human resources veteran who had witnessed firsthand the chaos families endured trying to find reliable domestic help. She built Anu&Co on a deceptively simple idea: trust must be earned before placement, not assumed after.

Starting with a team of 12 caregivers in Bangalore, we have grown organically — through word of mouth, 4.9 star reviews, and an unwavering refusal to compromise on verification standards — into a pan-India network covering 9 specialized service categories with over 1,200 active professionals.

Today, we serve over 5,000 families and corporates across Bangalore, Chennai, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Pune, and Delhi NCR — and counting. Every placement we make is backed by our multi layer vetting process, ongoing training, and a genuine commitment to the dignity and welfare of every professional in our network.

Our North Star

Mission & Vision

The guiding principles that shape every decision, placement, and interaction at Anu&Co.

Our Mission

To make quality home care and workforce solutions universally accessible, transparent, and reliable — by rigorously vetting every professional, continuously investing in their development, and standing behind every placement with an iron-clad service guarantee. We exist to give families the peace of mind they deserve, and to provide skilled professionals with dignified, sustainable employment.

Our Vision

To become India's definitive trusted platform for domestic and corporate manpower — a household name synonymous with safety, quality, and care. We envision a future where every family in India has frictionless access to vetted, professional home services, and where every skilled domestic worker has access to fair pay, career growth, and social recognition for their invaluable contribution.

What We Stand For

Our Four Core Values

These principles are not posters on a wall — they are embedded in every hire we make, every training we run, and every service we deliver.

Integrity

We operate with radical transparency. Our communication is honest and direct — clients and professionals alike always know exactly where they stand.

Compassion

We care deeply — about the families who entrust us with their loved ones, and about the professionals whose livelihoods depend on us. People always come first.

Excellence

Good enough is never good enough. Our professionals are trained, evaluated, and continuously developed to deliver service that genuinely exceeds expectations.

Safety

Safety is non-negotiable. Our multi-layer verification process, health screening, and ongoing monitoring systems ensure your home and family are always protected.

Safety First

Our Trust & Safety Framework

Every professional in the Anu&Co network clears a six-step vetting process before they are allowed to serve any client. We also conduct periodic re-assessments throughout their tenure. Your home security and family safety are never negotiable.

Identity Verification

Aadhaar, PAN, and residential address verification for every applicant

Police Clearance

Mandatory criminal background check issued by local police authorities

3 Reference Checks

Minimum three professional or personal references verified by our team

Medical Fitness

Health and fitness certification — including TB screening and blood tests

Skills Assessment

Practical skills test and interview by our in-house domain experts

Ongoing Monitoring

Regular client feedback reviews and periodic professional re-evaluations
