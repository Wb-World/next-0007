Common Questions

Booking FAQs

Quick answers to the most common questions we receive from clients before their first booking.

How quickly can a professional be arranged?

For most services, we can place a professional within 24–48 hours. Urgent placements (especially patient care) are often available same day. Call our emergency line for critical requirements.

Is there any advance payment or booking fee?

No advance payment is ever required to make a booking. Payment is only initiated once you've spoken to our team, confirmed the professional, and are satisfied with the match. Our pricing is fully transparent.

What if I'm not satisfied with the professional?

We offer a free, no-questions-asked replacement within 48 hours of any placement. Call our support line or WhatsApp us and we immediately begin sourcing a better match for you.

Are all professionals background verified?

Absolutely. Every professional clears Aadhaar verification, police clearance, three reference checks, a medical fitness test, and a practical skills assessment before they join our active network. We also conduct periodic re-verifications.

Can I specify preferences like gender, language, or religion?

Yes. We accommodate preferences for gender, language, dietary habits (vegetarian/non-vegetarian), religion, and more. Please mention requirements in the "Special Requirements" field or tell our team during the confirmation call.

Do you provide live-in professionals?

Yes — we provide live-in caregivers, nurses, cooks, and attendants. Please indicate whether you require live-in or live-out support when filling the booking form or during our confirmation call.
